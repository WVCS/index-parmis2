    Styling

    How you can make simple outfits using items above:

      1. Having neutral tank tops or shirts can be a good base to make your outfits around.
      2. You can pair the tank top with a baggy denim pants to make an everyday simple look.
      3. Having a jacket or a zip up is essential because you can ether dress it up with a leather jacket or dress it down with a simple zip up hoodie.

      Whats the point of Statement pieces?

      • Jewelry is essential for simple outfits to add a little accent and help look more put together and clean.
      • Adding accessories like a hat or glasses help bring the outfit together.
      • Bags aren't necessary but having few neutral and few over the top bags could help dress up the outfit.
